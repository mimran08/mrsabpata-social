Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-19 · Back

Summary

MrSabPata Auto Poster ("the App") is a single-user, first-party tool. It does not collect, store, or share personal data of TikTok users other than the @mrsabpata account owner.

Data Accessed

The App accesses only the following data from the @mrsabpata TikTok account, via OAuth tokens authorized by the account owner:

  • Open ID and display name (for verification that the connected account is correct).
  • Permission to publish video content (Content Posting API).

The App does not access videos belonging to other users, follower lists, direct messages, or any data outside the @mrsabpata account.

Data Storage

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored in the operator's private development environment and as GitHub Actions secrets used solely to authenticate scheduled posts. They are never shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App publishes to the @mrsabpata accounts on TikTok, Instagram, YouTube and X. Each platform applies its own privacy policy to the content published.

Data Retention & Deletion

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as needed for posting. Tokens are revoked by the operator by removing the App's authorization in TikTok account settings, after which the App can no longer access the account.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to anyone under 13. It does not knowingly collect data from minors.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to the @mrsabpata account.
